Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The game is free-to-play and is a very competitive and strategic game. Every player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits, making each and every battle more exciting and different.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite.

  • The game is very strategic and competitive, making the game more challenging and fun.
  • There are different ways to outplay your opponents.
  • You can play with your friends.
  • The mechanic of "micro" and "macro" skill in the game.
  • There is teamwork and I love watching how the professional players excel at the game.
